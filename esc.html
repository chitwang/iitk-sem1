ABOUT THE COURSE

  • The main motive of the course is to develop logic buliding. It is a compulsory course for all.

  • The lab sessions were held through Prutor.

  • My instructor: Dr. Swarnendu Biswas

  • There were a total of 11 labs out of which best 10 were considered. Each lab had a weightage of 7%. So, total weightage of labs = 70%. The remaining 30% comprised of 3 quizzes (10% each).

  • Below is the collection of all the practice , lab and quiz questions with their solutions. The answers are correct according to me (atleast the programs have passed all the test cases) , but better to verify yourself.

  • Before seeing solutions , try the questions yourself atleast once or twice.
